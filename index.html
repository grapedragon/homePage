Poison Ivy

About Poison Ivy

Ivy is great! She is a Batman villain but I like her anyway. She loves plants and has a doctorate in botany. She was poisoned by one of her professors and almost kill. But after the attempt on her life she gained incrediable powers.

Powers

  1. control over plants
  2. power to control pheromones
  3. immunity to all poisons
  4. enhanced strength and stamina
  5. to transfer poison through touch

Ivy's skin turned a green color after the attempt to kill her.
